Choosing the Right Stone & Design

Best Materials for Goa's Climate

Goa has heavy monsoons and salt air. Not all stones survive. We recommend:

  • Absolute Black Granite (Jet Black): The Gold Standard. Best for laser photo etching and gold lettering. Fade-proof.
  • Indian White Marble: Classic and serene. Requires regular cleaning but looks beautiful for Catholic angels and statues.
  • Red/Brown Granite: Excellent for deep-carved large letters. Very popular in South Goa cemeteries.

Respecting Every Faith

We are experienced in strict religious guidelines for gravestones:

  • Christian / Catholic: We carve precise Crosses, Angels, and "Rest in Peace" epitaphs. We also restore old Portuguese inscriptions.
  • Hindu: Custom Tulsi Vrindavan carving, Om symbols, and Sanskrit Shlokas on granite slabs for ancestors (Pitru).
  • Muslim: Traditional calligraphy and clean limestone or marble markers respecting Islamic traditions.
