Our Remote Service Workflow

We understand the anxiety of ordering from abroad. Here is how we give you peace of mind.

1. Video Consultation

We schedule a video call to show you stone samples and discuss designs face-to-face.

2. Digital Mockups

We send professional design proofs (PDF) for your approval before a single cut is made.

3. Progress Photos

You receive photo/video updates at key stages: raw stone selection, carving, and finishing.

4. Installation & Care

We handle cemetery permits and installation. We send a final geotagged video of the completed work.
